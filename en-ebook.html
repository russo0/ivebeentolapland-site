About Us

We are Alex and Naty Correia — a couple in love with the Northern Lights!
After years of traveling to Lapland chasing the phenomenon, we left everything behind in the Netherlands and moved to the heart of Swedish Lapland, where aurora hunting is now part of our routine (or, better said, our nights!).

We discovered that nothing is more magical than seeing the sky light up in green, pink, and red above snowy forests. That experience inspired us to create this e-book: to help more people make this dream come true, with real tips, detailed itineraries, and everything we learned in practice — now as locals in the Arctic.
