About Us

We are Alex and Naty Correia — a couple in love with the northern lights!
After years traveling back and forth to Lapland to see the aurora, we decided to leave everything behind in the Netherlands and move to the heart of Swedish Lapland, where aurora chasing became a part of our routine (or better said, our nights!).

Here, we discovered there’s no greater magic than seeing the sky light up in green, pink, and red above snowy forests. From this experience, our e-book was born: to help more people achieve this dream, with real tips, detailed itineraries and everything we learned in practice, now living as locals in the Arctic.
